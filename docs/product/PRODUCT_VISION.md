# Product Vision

## Purpose

Define the product intent for CoArchitect AI.

## Current Scope

CoArchitect AI is a workspace-centric architecture reasoning platform for the Microsoft Agents League hackathon.

## Problem Statement

Software architecture reviews are often inconsistent, slow, and hard to explain. Teams need a faster way to assess diagrams and architecture descriptions against standards, trade-offs, and decision quality without losing engineering judgment.

## Target Users

- engineering leads
- solution architects
- platform teams
- technical founders
- product and delivery teams preparing architecture decisions

See [PERSONAS.md](PERSONAS.md).

## Product Vision

CoArchitect AI acts as an AI architecture partner. It helps teams review architecture diagrams and descriptions through multi-agent reasoning, grounded knowledge, trade-off balancing, and ADR generation.

## Why Architecture Review Needs AI Agents

- architecture review is multi-step, not single-answer
- different standards emphasize different risks
- trade-offs need explanation, not only scoring
- decision records are easier to adopt when they are generated from evidence

## What Makes CoArchitect AI Different

- workspace-centric architecture collaboration
- standards-backed reasoning
- Foundry IQ-style intelligence layer
- visible agent reasoning trace
- application-owned Architecture Intelligence Score
- ADR generation tied to findings and trade-offs

## Product Principles

- keep architecture reasoning explainable
- separate AI suggestions from deterministic scoring
- optimize the demo for evaluator usability
- use synthetic data only for the hackathon
- keep current scope realistic and future scope clearly separated

## Value Proposition

CoArchitect AI helps teams move from architecture description to grounded review, prioritized findings, explicit trade-offs, and ADR-ready decisions in one coherent workflow.

## Future Enhancements

- real identity and RBAC
- richer collaboration history
- Azure AI Search-backed intelligence retrieval
- deeper ADR workflows and export automation
