# Demo Story

## Purpose

Describe the story the product tells during a live demo.

## Current Scope

The MVP story is about architecture reasoning, not enterprise administration.

## Story

CoArchitect AI is presented as an AI architecture partner.

The demo starts with a workspace and a synthetic architecture. The user adds a description, runs analysis, watches the reasoning stages, reviews the Architecture Intelligence Score, inspects grounded findings and trade-offs, and ends by generating an ADR.

## Why This Story Works

- it is easy to understand quickly
- it visibly demonstrates multi-step reasoning
- it shows Microsoft Foundry alignment without claiming more than the implementation delivers
- it ends with a practical artifact: the ADR

## Future Enhancements

Future demos can add richer history, multi-user collaboration, and enterprise knowledge connectors.
