# Personas

## Purpose

Summarize the primary users for the hackathon MVP.

## Current Scope

The MVP optimizes for technical evaluators and engineering practitioners.

## Persona 1 — Solution Architect

- needs a fast architecture review loop
- wants standards-backed findings
- values trade-off clarity

## Persona 2 — Engineering Lead

- needs a shared architecture quality signal
- wants recommendations that are practical
- wants ADR-ready output for team alignment

## Persona 3 — Platform Or Staff Engineer

- cares about reliability, observability, security, and scalability
- wants grounded critique, not generic advice

## Persona 4 — Hackathon Evaluator

- needs a fast setup experience
- wants visible reasoning and Microsoft technology alignment
- benefits from a short, explainable demo flow

## Future Enhancements

Future production personas may include compliance reviewers, delivery managers, and tenant administrators.
